EPIC HOMES MALAYSIA

I built a house in three days.

A hands-on volunteering experience in Kampung Orang Asli Chemperoh, Janda Baik.

01 BUILDING A HOME

From raw materials to a real home.

In July 2025, I joined 26 volunteers in Kampung Orang Asli Chemperoh, Janda Baik, to build a home for an Orang Asli family. Over three days, we transformed raw materials into a completed house, creating something that would directly impact a family's daily life.

02 TEAMWORK AND LEADERSHIP

Led by people who made everyone better.

Guided by EPIC Homes, our Master Builder, and experienced specialists, the project showed me the true meaning of teamwork. Every volunteer played a role, and the environment encouraged learning, collaboration, and growth.

03 BUILDING WITH PURPOSE

Every drop of sweat had meaning.

As part of the Wall Team, I worked alongside fellow volunteers to assemble and install key structural components of the house. Despite the physical demands and long hours under the sun, the shared commitment made every effort meaningful.

04 A CHANGED PERSPECTIVE

Impact is measured by people.

The most memorable moment was handing over the completed home and seeing the family's reaction. Working side by side with the Orang Asli community reshaped my understanding of community, gratitude, and what truly matters.
