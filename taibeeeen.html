Taibeeeen

A student-led initiative focused on supporting international students through community, guidance, social media communication, and practical information about studying in Malaysia.

Co-Founder Marketing Manager Social Media Strategy Student Support
01 / Overview

Built around a real student need.

Taibeeeen was created to help students who are planning to study in Malaysia by making the process easier to understand. The initiative focused on universities, English centres, student life, accommodation, and general guidance from a real student perspective.
My role focused on marketing direction, content planning, audience engagement, social media communication, and positioning the initiative in a way that attracted student attention before launch.
02 / My Role

Co-Founder & Marketing Manager.

03 / Campaign Direction

Curiosity before explanation.

Instead of revealing everything immediately, the campaign used teaser stories, countdown content, and audience questions to create curiosity around the initiative before the official launch.
